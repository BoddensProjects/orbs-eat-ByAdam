About the Game

This game was developed by Adam Boden for the 2024 Tech Fair at school. It's an exciting and interactive experience where players can use the spacebar to jump higher and the left/right arrow keys to move.

The goal is to collect orbs to score points before the timer runs out. A special green orb adds extra seconds to the timer. The game is a reflection of Adam's passion for programming.

During the Tech Fair, whoever achieves the highest score will win a prize. Good luck and have fun!
